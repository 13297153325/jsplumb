List items

Demonstrates how to connect items in lists. One list is configured with a jtk-scrollable-list attribute set, meaning jsPlumb finds it automatically. The other list is configured programmatically via an addList(..) call on the jsPlumb instance.

When you scroll a list and a connected item inside of it leaves the list's viewport, any connections to that item are stacked on the list element. When you use the `jtk-scrollable-list` attribute you cannot control the placement of the anchor or the appearance of the endpoint, but using `addList(..)` you can control both of these things.
